If this list gets too long, the system will automatically perform 'endless scrolling'

The browsers back/forward button will be working in a consistent way. Its also possible to refresh the whole page, or to bookmark it. (try it while editting a form for example)

For the special Relation-field look in the member/group demo and recursive demo.
